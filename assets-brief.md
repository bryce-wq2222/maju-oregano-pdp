- No kids or pregnant women anywhere in frame.
- Any text on graphic tiles uses ONLY copy we supply — no improvised claims, no "parasite," no disease names, no "proven."
- No AI-generated or AI-retouched-into-existence imagery. Real product, real people, disclosed stock only for sourcing frames.
